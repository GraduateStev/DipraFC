Meet the Team


Charles Leclerc

Driver since 2019

Leclerc is a very young talent, only arriving in the sport in 2018. Charles drove exceptionally for Alfa Romeo, which allowed him to join Ferrari in 2019, where he has quickly become the team's number one driver. Last year he finished 2nd, but he will be hungry to beat Max Verstappen in 2023.

Carlos Sainz

Driver since 2021

Carlos joined the Scuderia in his seventh season in F1. Though he is often outscored by his teammate, Carlos enjoys driving for Ferrari and even got his first win with the team in 2022. Carlos will be hoping to help his teammate win the title this season, and collect a few race wins himself.

Frédéric Vasseur

Team principal since 2023

Frédéric is a talented team principal with years of experience. 2023 will be his first year representing Ferrari, and it won't be easy. In 2022, Ferrari were plagued by tactical and driver errors and reliability issues. Vasseur knows the pressure is on him to finally bring the team a championship victory after 16 years.
